    SO, WHAT IS FIREAWAY?

    FIREAWAY Pizza is in a class all of its own. And rightly so.

    It is the modern foodies answer to a wholesome, guilt-free and ridiculously delicious, hand-kneaded crust. The secret is the 80% hydration of our propietary special blend of Italian flour. This ensures a crisp, light, easy-to-digest, healthier and tastier base!

    Nourishing, wholesome and completely irresistible, FIREAWAY is on a mission to introduce our uniquely healthy yet undeniably tasty menu to our community of thinkers, doers, movers, shakers and lovers of life.

    Founded in December 2015 as a Dubai home-grown concept, FIREAWAY founders Tamer and Faisal spent countless hours kneading, baking, and taste testing flour blends in Rome before landing on the signature FIREAWAY 30 hour-fermented base.

    We scoured Italy for the best cured meats such as our bresaola and beef spianata picante, our Italian tomatoes, and Italian cheeses.

    Our mozzarella is locally produced by the finest Italian craftsmen and our vegetables are sourced locally when possible, ensuring that we select only the freshest GMO-free veggies and herbs for our toppings.

    In 2015, FIREAWAY opened its first site in Business Bay, Dubai and since has successfully grown to 6 further locations across the Emirate with a further location in neighbouring Abu Dhabi.

    Between 2018-2021 we also operated 2 stores in London, UK. 2021 also brought our expansion across the Middle East with the first pop-up locations in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia that operated for a 12 month duration.

    With these two international markets operating successfully, FIREAWAY is now planning further expansion to become a globally recognised brand.

    2022 has seen FIREAWAY grow even further with our first dine-in locations in Gate Avenue, DIFC, Dubai and Marina Mall, Abu Dhabi.

    With all tastes and flavours covered, and the choice of original or gluten-free crusts, we guarantee you will always be coming back for more.

    LESS CALORIES FOR YOUR DOUGH

    A question we get asked all the time is what is in your dough?. We never have and never will keep secrets about any of our ingredients, so here is the lowdown on our dough.

    We use a unique blend of flour created specifically for FIREAWAY! a blend of Naturally Dried Sourdough, Soy Flour and Fiber, Rice Flour and Soft Whole Wheat Flour. This blend gives us just the right amount of crunch in the crust but maintains a soft and fluffy centre.

    On top of that, we use 80% water to 20% flour and only the tiniest amount of yeast and gluten meaning you can say goodbye to that bloated feeling youd probably get eating (entire) other Pizzas.

    Oh, and all our flour is Organic, GMO-Free with absolutely  ZERO chemical additives!
